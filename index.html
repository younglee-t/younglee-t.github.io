Biography

I completed a PhD from the London School of Economics where my thesis looked at some problems in entropy minimizer for general stochastic processes. This is me here.

I then worked for the finance industry in London for about 5 years. After realizing that I am more interested in research than the industry, I took up a position as a Researcher at NICTA/Data61 in 2015 and have been looking at some problems in inference for stochastic processes.

Research Interests

Semi-martingales, neural point processes, differential neural computing
